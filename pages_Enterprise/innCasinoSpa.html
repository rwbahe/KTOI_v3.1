Kootenai River Inn Casino & Spa

In 1986, the Kootenai River Inn opened on Kootenai Tribe lands overlooking the gorgeous Kootenai River. Carrying the Best Western Brand, it began with 47 guest rooms with river view balconies, a Tribal gift shop, restaurant, meeting rooms and indoor recreation center. In 1995, the Tribe celebrated the opening of the Kootenai Casino, and has since added three more casino rooms, guest rooms, a luxury Spa, and larger recreation center.

The latest expansion is the 36-room Kootenai Falls Lodge, with luxury amenities including walk-in glass shower, extra large flat screen TVs, refrigerator, microwave and desks in select rooms. With this project, the Kootenai Rover Inn Casino and Spa was honored with the coveted Best Western Design Champion Award.

Funding from this enterprise contributes to the Tribal health clinic, college scholarships for Tribal members, funding to local schools and seed money for other Tribal projects.
