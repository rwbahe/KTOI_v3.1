Finance

The Finance department oversee a wide range of Tribal affairs, ranging from financial services and working with the Gaming Commission to economic development, housing, transportation, Indian Child Welfare, Tribal Employment Rights, Tribal Court and community liaison efforts. These offices work closely with the Tribal Council to promote economic development for Tribal enterprises.

The departments economic focus includes working with the local community to develop environmentally responsible new jobs, create a fair profit for the Tribe and help the rural economy diversify and prosper.
