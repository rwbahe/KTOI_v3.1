Health Clinic

Opened in 1993, the Kootenai Tribal Health Clinic offers comprehensive patient centered health care.

Services include:

  • Physician, Nurse Practitioner, Registered Nurse, Dietitian and Patient Care Coordinator appointments
  • Behavioral and mental health visits with certified counselors
  • Routine and basic screening labs
  • Immunizations
  • Chiropractic care
  • Well child and routine physical exams
  • Referrals to dental, optical and specialized care as needed
